About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio. My name is Nia Smith and I am a sophmore at the University of Maryland. I am a journalism major with a minor in general bussines and I am also apart of the Media, Self and Society Scholars program. In this program we look into how media impacts society and learn tools to help up navigate the internet. I am still trying to figure out what I want to do in the future, but I am really interested in broadcast journalism, pop-culture and entertainment journalsim and sports journalism. Thank you for visiting my website and checking out some of my work.
